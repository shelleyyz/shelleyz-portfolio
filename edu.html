Overview

Make ADHD your bitch is a potential training solution designed as a resource for adults diagnosed with attention deficit hyperactive disorder.

This is a passion project of mine that I worked on as part of a UX design course, with the objective of coming up with an approach or solution to support lifelong management of attention deficit hyperactive disorder symptoms.

Opportunity statement

Opportunity statement

"How might we provide a potential solution or approach for adults diagnosed with ADHD so that they feel supported in their social, work and personal lives?"

Background

There are four key painpoints that people with ADHD experience:

  • Recognizing the disorder
  • Getting a diagnosis
  • Accessing treatment
  • Difficulties with ongoing life management
